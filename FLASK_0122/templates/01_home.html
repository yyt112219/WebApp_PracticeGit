About our data source

WIPO - World Intellectual Property Organization.Headquartered in Geneva, Switzerland, WIPO is one of the 15 specialized agencies of the United Nations (UN) which was created to promote and protect intellectual property (IP) across the world. Currently, WIPO has 193 member states and contains more than 90 million patent documents, which requires certain amount of knowledge in big data technology for analysis.
The International Patent Classification (IPC) system is an effective and worldwide tool for classifying and searching patent applications. It divides technology fields into 8 major sections (A-H) with several subdivisions and subgroups. The following IPC scheme table is provided for your reference.

What We Do

The main goal of this project is to create a detailed and multi-dimensional patents database using the data we crawled on WIPO official website. With the database we created, we trained several ML models and built a patent classification system. We also provided an interactive dashboard with some informative descriptive statistics graphs on our webpage, aiming to assist the users with their research on patents-related studies.
